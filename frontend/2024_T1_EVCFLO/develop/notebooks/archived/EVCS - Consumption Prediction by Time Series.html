# Sort values by "Start Date"
df = df.sort_values(by = ["Start Date"])
df["Start Date"] = pd.to_datetime(df["Start Date"]).dt.date
df["End Date"] = pd.to_datetime(df["End Date"]).dt.date
df["Start"] = pd.to_datetime(df["Start Time"])
df["End"] = pd.to_datetime(df["End Time"])
df["Start mins"] = df["Start"].dt.hour*60 + df["Start"].dt.minute
df["End mins"] = df["End"].dt.hour*60 + df["End"].dt.minute
df["Time Period"] = df["End mins"] - df["Start mins"]
df = df[["Connector", "Start Date", "Start Time", "End Date", "End Time", "Time Period", "Total kWh", "Site", "Model"]]

Future work would include feeding the most updated datasets into our time-series model and making predictions for the coming year. Such a process would enable us or our future clients to estimate the total energy consumption in a period of time that can satisfy all EV user demands on their networks. Another research point would be how we judge on total energy consumption in one EVCS would reach a threshold that requires for creation of another EVCS in the same region.¶
